How to Play

A player will have 10 attempts to guess the number combinations made by the computer

The computer will give you some hints to help you out

You have 10 seconds to get the correct number combinations

Example:

These three icons are associated with a numerical place in the random generated numbers
❌✔️☑️

✔️ means that the number was in the right spot, ☑️ means one number is in the random combination but not in the right spot, and ❌ means that the numbers are not in the combination.

Press the start button to start the game.
